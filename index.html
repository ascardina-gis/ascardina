Populations changes in Argentina from 1970-1980

The smallest changes in population in the 1970s were on the east coast, and the provinces with the next smallest population changes were in the western provinces. Central Argentina seems to have changed the most, which is interesting because much of those provinces are devoted to farms and ranches.

Maximum water capacity of Minnesotan dams

There is a general trend of smaller to larger dams from south to north on the map, although the majority of dams are quite small (20k or less) and scattered relatively evenly throughout the state. It is also interesting to note the several large dams (200k or more) on the western border of the state.

Average family sizes and rates of home ownership in Minneapolis

There is a clear delineation between the smaller tracts around the center of the metropolitan area, and the larger tracts on the outskirts. On the outskirts there are many large areas of households that are both large and owner-occupied, where as in the center the tracts are more likely to be large and rented, small and owner, or small and rented. This probably has to do with high real estate prices closer to downtown.
